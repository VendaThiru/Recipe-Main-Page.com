Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This clasic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your of choice of cheese,vegitables, or meats.

Preparation Time
  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings:cheese,diced vegitables,cokked meats,herbs

Instructions

  1. 1.Beat the eggs:In a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. 1.Heat the pan:Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. 1.Cook the omelette:Once the butter is melted and bubbling,pour in the eggs.Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. 1.Add fillings(optional):When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle,sprinle yourchosen filling over one half of the omelete.
  5. 1.Fold and serve:As the omelette continue to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings.Let it cook for another minutes,then slide it onto a plate.
  6. 1.Enjoy:Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
